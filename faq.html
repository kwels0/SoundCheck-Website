FAQ?

Can I book a room on the same day? +
Yes, subject to availability. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for guaranteed slots.
Are there any discounts for long bookings? +
Yes, we offer discounts for bookings of 5+ hours. Contact us for custom rates.
Can I bring my own gear? +
Absolutely! You're welcome to bring your instruments or use our available equipment.
What are the operating hours of the rehearsal studio? +
Our studio operates from 12:00 PM to 9:00 PM daily.
What equipment is included in the rehearsal studio? +
Each room is equipped with drums, amplifiers, microphones, a PA system, and a basic mixer.
